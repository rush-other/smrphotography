Yes I Am!

Inquiring minds have wanted to know, so I've added a new question to the "Frequently Asked Questions" section of the Portrait of a Vegan page:

Are you vegan?

Yes! I am proud to say I've been vegan for 6 years (as of May 2008), and was vegetarian for about 9 years before that.

This project gives me the chance to blend two of my passions: Veganism and photography.

Tuesday, March 11, 2008

Portrait of a Vegan

My current project, "Portrait of a Vegan," is about 6 weeks old now and is already such a success thanks to the wonderful vegan community here in the Portland area.

Already 37 make that 38 vegans have contacted me, and most have already had their portraits taken or are on the schedule for the near future. I am overjoyed with how thoughtful all of the participants are with their portrait location choices and questionnaire responses. They are making this project a real pleasure for me and I can't thank each and every one of them enough!
